Meet Saige

My Biography!

As a teenager, I always had a passion for photography and I’ve had a camera in my hand ever since. I am self taught and continue to grow my experience every day.

I strive to take amazing photos offering a story you can share with family and friends. I do this by making each session a one of a kind experience, by offering different types of live scenery backdrops, posing options and clothing changes. Editing is one of my favorite parts and plays a big part in transforming your still photographs into your beautiful story. While I work to accommodate each of your photography desires, I am always open to new suggestions, ideas and challenges. So please don't hesitate to ask.

I would like to thank everyone who has supported me, and continue to support me in my love for photography. None of this would be possible without you!

Xoxo,

Saige Rios
